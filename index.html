Math & Stuff

This site is about the Math, Science and Software I have had the opportunity to learn and use. This covers the period from the early days of the space program at Cape Kennedy/Canaveral to today. I thought it would be interesting to present because it is not readily available and may be of interest, even if only for historical purposes. Some of the major topics are:

  • Least Squares and error modeling.
  • Real Time Software Development.
  • Refractive effects on optical and radar tracking systems.
  • MatLab Development.
  • Web Development.
